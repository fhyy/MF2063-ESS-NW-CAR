\section*{General Description of Status}
Since October 10th, when the project plan was completed, work has carried on
according to plan. The project is nearing the end of the prototyping phase and
the actuators, sensors and camera systems are up and running and what remains
of the prototyping phase is to interconnect those systems.

\section*{Resource Status}
The resource status is good. The actual resources spent were less than planned
at the time of writing. The EVM graph can be seen in Appendix A.

\section*{Problems / Action Plan}
No problems have been identified at the moment of writing.

\section*{Risks / Action Plan}
The risk management is mostly being carried out as expected. Risks 1, 2 and 4 are
expected to become more prominent within the coming weeks when the different
subsystems are being interconnected. At the time of writing proactive measures
are in effect for these risks. Proactive measure for Risk 3 is in effect and
team members are sticking to their independent fields. The documentation tool
for Risk 5 has not yet been integrated into the project.

\section*{Project Changes From the Project Plan}
No official documents have been removed or changed at the time of
writing. A resource plan has been added. Official documents now consist of the
project plan with appendices, the risk analysis and the newly added resource
plan. There have been no changes to the organizational structure of the
project.
